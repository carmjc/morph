Welcome to the Morph RTS.

The goal of this project is to craft a nice RTS game with the following guiding principles:

  • Morphs can do a limited amount of things.
  • However, they can be assembled in a infinite number of combinations.
  • Any unit in the RTS is composed of one or several morphs assembled as requested by the player. The player can use patterns to avoid designing very big units by hand every time.
  • Units can be IA assembled and IA driven on the battlefield, IA can be programmed by the player.

Project state

A basic engine is available to see the general idea of the project. You can fork the project or inject ideas as issues in the issue database.
